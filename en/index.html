What we do

MAINTENANCE
We provide service for forklifts, pallet trucks, electric platform trucks and trucks for lightweight transport. We offer major and current repairs and maintenance inspections. We also replace exploitative parts and replenish working fluids.
SALES
Our range of products:
  • spare parts
  • used and new forklifts
  • tires
  • oils and filters
  • storage and traction batteries
We have a possibility to import parts on customer wish.
SUPERVISION
We provide services in the field of full-year maintenance for UDT and execute acceptance tests. We prepare trucks for receiving UDT, as well as offer assistance in the registration procedure. Our conservators commute to facilities of customers within agreed terms.
